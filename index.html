Business Understanding.

Omidian monitors and insures solar panel performance for residents and businesses across the company. They model expected output for each asset (solar panel generation site) and compare it with the measured output. If a panel underperforms they send one of their service partners to address the issue.

Business Understanding Omnidian monitors solar panel performance across the country,
generally using data gathered from the transformers on site. This yields time series data, which is compared with
expected output (modeled from location, weather, etc.). When there is a large difference between the two,
Omnidian dispatches one of its contracted companies to go fix it. Different problems may call for different fixes,
and different contractors may have different specialties. Would an electrician be better equipped to handle this,
or would a solar panel technician? On the other hand, this may be something the customer is responsible for; they may just need to clean their roof.
I built a model to categorize common problems to help inform the decision making process. While there is no substitute for good judgement, good statistical models can be very helpful.
